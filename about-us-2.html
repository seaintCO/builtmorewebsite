Our History

A Legacy of Commercial Construction Excellence

Founded on the principles of hard work, uncompromising safety, and absolute transparency, Built-More LLC has grown from a regional builder into a highly sought-after national commercial contractor. With deep roots in the Nashville, TN area (615), our operational footprint now spans across the continental United States.

We specialize in highly technical, fast-track commercial projects including multi-unit restaurant franchises, high-end retail buildouts, state-of-the-art fitness centers, and corporate office facilities. Our team understands that in commercial construction, time is revenue. We engineer our schedules and manage our supply chains to ensure your doors open on time, every time.

Our leadership team brings decades of combined field and management experience. We don't just oversee projects; we actively drive them. By maintaining a network of elite subcontractors and adhering to rigorous internal quality controls, Built-More ensures that the final product exceeds architectural specifications and client expectations alike.
