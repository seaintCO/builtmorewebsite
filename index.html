Built-More, LLC is a full-service general contractor specializing in commercial construction nationwide. We have built a reputation based on integrity, quality, and superior customer service.

Our Legacy

A Foundation Built on the Golden Rule

Founded in 2002 as a second-generation family business, Built-More operates strictly by the Golden Rule: "Do unto others as you would have done unto you." This ethical cornerstone guides our nationwide commercial construction services, which encompass new construction, remodeling, and complex tenant build-outs.

Our principals, project managers, and superintendents bring years of advanced education and hands-on experience to the field. We take immense pride in the fact that our portfolio of hundreds of completed projects relies heavily on repeat customers and professional referrals—a true testament to our reliability from conception to completion.

Whether managing a small business improvement or a large corporate rollout, we adapt to our clients' precise needs. We participate in design-build, fixed-price, GMP, and negotiated contracts to deliver maximum value engineering on every project.
